Short Story Long #43

The quasi-daily not-for-profit newspaper of New New Cheeseland | Issue 43, 09/09/22

Politics: Green Socialist Ferrat Party announce their new manifesto

The newly formed Green Socialist Ferrat Party, formed from the merger of the Ferret, Green Anarchist Rat and Separatist Socialist Union Parties, announced its first official manifesto yesterday. The manifesto includes numerous major policies, such as pursuing media regulation, standardised polygon naming, establishing a state-owned school, and sorting out the Discord server and administrative divisions. This article aims to explain their manifesto to help you make up your mind on who you will vote for in the presidential election tomorrow, and which party you should be in.

Media Regulation

The party will establish a new ministry, the Ministry for the Regulation of Media Outlets (MoRMO), which will run a system of media licenses. An outlet must possess such such a license in order to legally operate.

Educational Reform

A 'Cheeselandic National School' will be established. This will be responsible for providing a high-quality education on Cheeselandic history and politics to all citizens, and will be funded and owned by the government through taxes.

Discord Server

The party aims to clean up the New New Cheeseland Discord server by restoring the original channel categories, reinstating key channels from the 'deleted archived channels' category. This is a policy that is a the heart of Alan, the party leader, 's presidential campaign.

Administrative Divisions

The party will 'sort out the country's administrative divisions', which will likely include rectifing the constitutional loophole created by the migration of the nation's population from the pond to the flagstones outside the Walker Building in some way. Alan, who initially proposed the policy, has stated "This will not be done through a constitutional amendment, so it is constitutional".

Other Policies

The party also aims to ban the following actions:

  • Pushing into a queue.
  • Harming or killing a ferret (or ferrat).

The Chaos Party's Policies

The Chaos Party has not announced an official manifesto, so Short Story Long is assuming that they will continue to use their old manifesto, which includes the following policies:

  • Establish a Ministry of Health to provide plasters to those citizens who need them.
  • Create dedicated channels for gaming discussion and debates.

Business: Louis launches 'Cheese Publishing' to replace Wekkly Chese

Louis, creator of the epic website cheese.animateshadows.org, has launched 'Cheese Publishing', which will publish the Wekkly Chese, now written by Alan, as well as a Weekly summary of the week's Geography lessons and homework co- written by James and Louis. Short Story Long is also planning on entering into a contract with Cheese Publishing to have the newspaper hosted on the site, but will still continue to post in #short-story-long, and will remain not-for-profit and independent. The new website is 'minimal' according to its creator, but still possesses a 'light' and 'dark' theme, with a third theme, 'twilight' also planned.

Politics: New New Cheeseland remains silence on death of Queen

The New New Cheeseland government has yet to make any comment on the death of notable world leader Elizabeth II. Alan has theorised that this is due to New New Cheeseland having declared independence from Britain in the recent past, and as such not wanting to make a potentially controversial statement on the passing. Other theories include an anti-monarchist stance created by the revolution against the authoritarian rule of the King of Honse, or the fact New New Cheeseland has yet to recognise the existence of any other world leader, and would largely have little to say about the Queen.
